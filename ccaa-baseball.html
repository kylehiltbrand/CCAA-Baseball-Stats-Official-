📖 Stat Glossary & Formulas ▼
Advanced metrics computed from base stats following standard sabermetric formulas.
Hitting
AVGBatting Average — H / AB
OBPOn-Base % — (H+BB+HBP) / (AB+BB+HBP+SF)
SLGSlugging % — Total Bases / AB
OPSOn-Base + Slugging (OBP + SLG)
ISO ADVIsolated Power — SLG − AVG (extra-base hit power)
BABIP ADVBatting Avg on Balls in Play — (H−HR) / (AB−K−HR+SF)
wOBA ADVWeighted On-Base Avg — weights each outcome by run value (BB×.69, 1B×.89, 2B×1.27, 3B×1.62, HR×2.10)
wRC+ ADVWeighted Runs Created Plus — park/league-adjusted wRC, 100 = average, >100 above average
oWAR ADVOffensive WAR — wins above replacement from batting + baserunning. Includes no defensive value. Based on wRAA + SB/CS runs + replacement adjustment (~20R/600PA).
BB%Walk Rate — BB / PA
K%Strikeout Rate — K / PA
BB/KWalk-to-Strikeout Ratio — BB / K
XBHExtra-Base Hits — 2B + 3B + HR
Pitching
ERAEarned Run Average — (ER × 9) / IP
WHIPWalks + Hits per Inning Pitched — (BB+H) / IP
K/9Strikeouts per 9 innings — (K × 9) / IP
BB/9Walks per 9 innings — (BB × 9) / IP
H/9Hits per 9 innings — (H × 9) / IP
HR/9Home Runs per 9 innings — (HR × 9) / IP
K/BBStrikeout-to-Walk Ratio — K / BB
K%Strikeout Rate — K / batters faced
BB%Walk Rate — BB / batters faced
pWAR ADVPitcher WAR — wins above replacement based on ERA vs. league average. Rep level ≈ 1.5× lgERA. Positive = better than a replacement-level pitcher.
FIP ADVFielding Independent Pitching — (13×HR + 3×BB − 2×K) / IP + 3.10. ERA-like scale, removes defense.
xFIP ADVExpected FIP — normalizes HR rate to league average fly-ball HR%. More predictive than FIP.
BABIP ADVBatting Avg on Balls in Play against pitcher. Average ~.300; higher may indicate bad luck.
